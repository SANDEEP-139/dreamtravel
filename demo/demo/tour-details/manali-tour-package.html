Manali Tour Of 4 Days 3 Nights From Delhi:

Description

Manali, a magnetic town nestled in the Himalayas beckons to the soul of adventures, nature lovers as well as solace-seekers. Stretched out leisurely on the banks of the River Beas, this charming hill station is blessed with a magnificent scenery characterized by forests, valleys, streams, rivers and orchards that has increased its popularity faster than the clouds that dance on its hilltops. Explore Manali on foot, or try paragliding over its rolling green slopes and apple orchards. Wonderfully combining nature and adventure, it is serenity, history, nature, spirituality and shopping, all merged into one. A potpourri of cultures and experiences, Manali is the perfect mountain getaway for everyone.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day : 1 Arrival & Sightseeing in Manali

    Delhi - Manali Volvo (Overnight):Overnight Journey Delhi to Manali

    Reach a predetermined spot in Delhi where a representative will greet you and help you with the smooth transfer from Delhi to Manali. The overnight journey takes you through some of the most scenic views as you travel to the majestic Himalayas to reach Manali the next morning. Upon arrival in Manali, check-in at the hotel. Later in the afternoon, proceed on a half-day city tour and visit the Hongkong Market and the Tibetan Monastery. Explore the Shopping malls of Manali and enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day : 2 Manali to Solang (Day Trip to Solang Valley / Snow Point)

    Enjoy a hearty breakfast at the hotel and later get ready for an excursion to Solang Valley which is a picturesque setting. Indulge in some adventure sports like Snow Scooter, Skiing, Parachuting, Paragliding, and Zorbing at Solang. In the evening, Experience the vast and majestic hill roads covered in snow that gives a jaw-dropping view along with sparkling rivers that flow.

  • Day : 3 Manali to Manikaran via kullu

    After breakfast, check out of your hotel in Manali and drive to Manikaran, which is situated about 75 kms from Kullu. Manikaran is essentially a sacred pilgrimage place, especially for the Hindus. It is believed that the Lord Manu recreated human life in Manikaran after the severe flood. The place is replete with several temples and a gurudwara. After the sightseeing in Manikaran, drive to Kullu for a half day sightseeing tour. Dinner and overnight stay at Manali.

  • Day : 4 Manali to Hadimba Temple end travel to Delhi

    • The every time is best time to visit the temple However, this can tend to change because Himachal Pradesh has one the most unpredictable and bizarre climatic conditions every year. The temple is locally known as the Dhungiri Temple because it is situated inside the Dhungiri Forest.
    • The Hadimba Devi is a Mountain Deity or a Goddess of the Forest.
    • After hadimba devi temple then move on towards your bus from manali to delhi by relaxing in Volvo bus.
