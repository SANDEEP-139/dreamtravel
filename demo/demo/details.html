Neil Island, point Tour Packages 5

Description

A tiny island located to the south of Andaman is Neil Island, brimming with less explore marine diversity, coral reefs, long beaches and bewitching greenery. Being one of the tourist destinations in Andaman, Neil is so happy to become the host with revealing all that it has. A romantically silent island unlike the hustle of Havelock Island, Neil Island is termed as the vegetable bowl of Andaman. .

Go sliding down the slopes of Solang valley or shop your heart out at the local market, Neil Island has it all. Explore the town of Kullu that is wonderfully blessed with a combination of adventure and nature. At the end pass through the Mini Israel of India - Kasol to reach Manikaran, wherein you can take a relaxing dip in the hot water springs, marking the perfect end of your holiday in the mountains.

Detailed Day Wise Itinerary

  • Day : 1 Arrival & Sightseeing in Neil Island

    Reach a predetermined spot in Delhi where a representative will greet you and help you with the smooth transfer from Delhi to Neil Island. The overnight journey takes you through some of the most scenic views as you travel to the majestic Himalayas to reach Neil Island the next morning. Upon arrival in Neil Island, check-in at the hotel. Later in the afternoon, proceed on a half-day city tour and visit the Hidimba Devi Temple, Vashist Hot Water Springs, Jogni waterfalls,  Van Vihar, Hongkong Market and the Tibetan Monastery. Explore the Shopping malls of Neil Island and enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day : 2 Neil Island to Kasol Sightseeing

    Wake up and treat your taste buds with a yummy breakfast at the hotel and later get ready for an excursion to Kasol and vsist the Kheerganga, Manikaran Sahib, Moon Dance Café. After a day full of thrill and excitement, return back to the hotel for a hearty dinner. Overnight stay in Neil Island.

     

  • Day : 3 Neil Island to Solang (Day Trip to Solang Valley / Snow Point)

    Enjoy a hearty breakfast at the hotel and later get ready for an excursion to Solang Valley which is a picturesque setting. Indulge in some adventure sports like Snow Scooter, Skiing, Parachuting, Paragliding, and Zorbing at Solang. In the evening, board your transfer for Noida. Experience the vast and majestic hill roads covered in snow that gives a jaw-dropping view along with sparkling rivers that flow. Overnight journey to Noida.
